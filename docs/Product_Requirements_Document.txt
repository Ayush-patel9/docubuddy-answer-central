Title: AI Interview Coach - PRD
Date: July 15, 2025
Owner: Product Manager

Summary:
The AI Interview Coach will simulate mock interviews using AI avatars. It will give real-time feedback on tone, speed, and expression. Users can upload resumes, select interview types (tech, HR, pitch), and receive a full report after the session.

Goals:
- Improve candidate interview readiness
- Provide analytics per session
- Include mock investor pitch option
